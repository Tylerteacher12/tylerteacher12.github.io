Improve your pronunciation with the Mimicry Method

Tyler Clock 25/02/2022 0

Why pronunciation is important

What do babies, gray parrots, and successful language learners with good pronunciation have in common? They learn to pronounce words using mimicry. Of course, the way that parrots understand language may be very different than humans. But mimicry remains a powerful tool for improving your pronunciation.

Pronunciation is one of the most important and most difficult aspects of learning a new language. Understanding the sounds of a language allows us both to understand and be understood.

Many language teachers don’t emphasize pronunciation in their classes, because they argue that we should not want to hide our accents. While I agree that there is nothing wrong with having an accent, pronunciation errors can sometimes lead to confusion. This is why many students insist on learning pronunciation.

Some non-native English speakers who learned through textbooks and traditional language classes can read and write very well, but struggle with speaking and listening because they have not had the opportunity to focus on pronunciation. The purpose of this article is to introduce the Mimicry Method which is one of several ways to improve your pronunciation.

The Mimicry Method

The Mimicry Method is actually a very traditional way to improve your pronunciation. You can imagine a traditional classroom where a teacher brings in a stereo. The teacher might play an audio recording one sentence at a time while students repeat each sentence.

Nowadays, students can find authentic materials to practice online. We can use real world materials rather than professional audio recordings to improve our pronunciation. We can even choose what accent we would like to practice by finding an actor, influencer, or public figure that we would like to sound like.

What does the Mimicry Method look like? First, choose a speaker to mimic. This could be a native speaker whose accent you like, or a non-native speaker from your country of origin. It should be a clear accent that you would like to emulate.

Once you have someone that you would like to sound like, it’s time to practice. Find a clip of this person on Youtube or another social media platform where they are speaking English. Listen closely to one sentence at a time. Repeat the sentence as they say it. You will monitor your speech to hear how similar it is to the speaker.

You can improve on this technique by recording yourself speaking. A voice recording application or even an application like tiktok that allows you to pause and resume recording will be needed. Record your voice as you repeat your chosen speaker. When you finish, listen to your recording and note any errors that you hear yourself making. Repeat the activity to improve on these mistakes.

A note on choosing a non-native speaker from your country.

Sometimes, we don’t want to get rid of our accents completely. Many native speakers think foreign accents are pleasant and attractive. So you might want to choose a celebrity from your country to mimic, if you would like to retain your (beautiful) accent but be easier to understand. Just make sure it is someone that is easily understood by native speakers, like an actor or celebrity who has done work or interviews in English.

How a tutor can help you improve pronunciation

Pronunciation is complicated. It’s common to make errors in a new language based on your mother tongue. For example, Spanish has a rolled /r/ sound that doesn’t exist in English. While I can make this sound, it’s not always easy for me and sometimes sounds funny to Spanish speakers. It takes a lot of practice to get right.

A tutor can help you to identify the errors that cause miscommunications. If they have experience teaching pronunciation, they can help you to isolate these sounds by showing you how they are produced. Once you identify the problem, you can practice making the correct sound and even using it in context. A tutor can help make this process more precise and memorable.
