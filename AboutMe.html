About Me

Tyler Clock 08/02/2022 0

My name is Tyler and I have dedicated myself to teaching English since 2014. My career began in St. Paul, Minnesota, where I decided to gain experience teaching with the Minnesota Literacy Council. To be honest, teaching was difficult in those early days. The first time that I walked into a classroom was terrifying. But I stuck with it, and this career has taken me to amazing places like South Korea and Mexico. I love my job because I get to meet people from around the world, and help them to reach their goals.

In 2020, I decided to start teaching online full time. I think online learning is the future, and I want to be the best online English teacher. When you learn online, you can learn from the comfort of your own home. You can access so many resources nowadays. It is so much easier than it was in the past to learn a language. I love connecting students with exciting resources. It is very rewarding to see students improve their language skills and really express themselves in English.

My own history as a language learner has not always been easy. I failed Spanish in high school, but now I can communicate with native speakers with confidence. I understand the language learning process, but only after devoting several years to learning about it. In 2018, I decided to pursue a masters degree in Applied Linguistics. It was during that time that I learned about how we learn langages. I also learned about how language teachers can help their students to stay motivated during the learning process. I hope what I have learned can be useful to people who want to learn English to improve their lives.
