About Me

Tyler 08/02/2022

Hello there! It's nice to meet you. My name is Tyler. :)

I'm an English teacher who loves making class fun for my students. I love seeing my students get excited about learning and communicating.

Here is my story: In 2016, I graduated with a degree in English literature and a TEFL certificate. I wanted to travel the world, so I started teaching in South Korea at a private English academy. I met many amazing students there. I liked teaching so much that I decided to study for my master's degree in Applied Linguistics and TESOL. I finished my degree in 2019 after doing research on how to motivate young learners to learn a new language.

In 2018, I moved to Mexico to teach at an international school. It has been an amazing experience for me. I learned about the IB curriculum and how to help my students learn independently. During my time here, I have had many amazing experiences and became fluent in Spanish.

Now, it's 2022. I specialize in teaching English online. After taking online Spanish classes, I am truly convinced that online classes can be the best way to learn a language. There are so many online resources for us to use, and all from the comfort of your home.

Thanks for reading! I hope to meet you soon. :)
