How to Learn English with Podcasts

Tyler Clock 08/02/2022 0

Did you know that you can improve your language skills while washing the dishes, walking your dog or driving your car? Podcasts may be the answer for the busy language-learner. Researchers have known the benefits of podcasts on language learning for many years. A 2013 meta-analysis published in the journal English Language Teaching highlights how podcasts can be useful for language learners. The article by Masudul Hasan and Tan Bee Hoon, researchers from Malaysia, explains how podcasts can be beneficial for listening, speaking, grammar, and pronunciation skills.

Many of the authors cited in the literature review found strong evidence to support using podcasts for learning a language. Podcasts are an example of an extended listening activity. These activities challenge learners to engage with the new language for long periods of time. The article cites several studies that showed that podcasts improved students scores on listening exams. There are two reasons that podcasts can improve a students listening ability. The first is that students encounter many new vocabulary words and grammatical forms when listening to a podcast. The second is that podcasts allow students to improve their phonetic awareness; this means they are better able to recognize the sounds of the target language. Because many students don't have the opportunity to practice speaking and listening in traditional classes, podcasts are a great way to grow these skills independently.

In my own experience, I have found podcasts incredibly useful for learning a language. When I arrived to Mexico, I knew very little Spanish. Because I was starting a new job as an English teacher and most of my coworkers spoke English, I didn't have as many opportunities to practice my Spanish as I would have liked. This is where podcasts came in. They fit perfectly into my busy schedule because I could listen while doing other activities. I learned a lot about Mexican culture through podcasts as well. My recommendation for anyone trying to improve their language skills is to find podcasts about any subject they are interested in, and try out a few until you find one that you like. Don't worry if you can't understand everything right away. Try to listen for context clues. The content will become more clear the more that you immerse yourself in it.
